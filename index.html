Day-to-Day Mindset How I think and make decisions
🌓

Bridge engineering & business

I translate both ways — turning technical realities into business language, and business needs into precise engineering requirements. Neither side should feel lost.

"What does this mean for the user?" and "What does this cost to build?" — asked in the same meeting.
🛡

Responsible AI from day one

Ethics, GDPR compliance, and explainability aren't afterthoughts — they're designed in from the first discovery session. Trust is a product feature, not a legal checkbox.

Every AI system I build includes a human-in-the-loop mechanism and a clear explanation of how decisions are made.
🔍

Discovery first, always

I never start with a solution. I start with the problem. User interviews, journey maps, and pain point analysis come before any wireframe or roadmap.

The best product decisions I've made came from one user saying something I didn't expect in an interview.
